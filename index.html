AI Assurance & Data Governance

Desmond Mariita

I audit AI systems for trustworthiness: fairness, robustness, recourse, and whether their explanations are faithful. I also govern the data behind them. The output is evidence: reproducible tests, a signed self-assessment opinion, and claims that trace to artifacts.

Every writeup here links back to the runnable, tagged repository that produced its numbers. The writeups are the narrative; the repos are the proof.

Primary repository

eu-ai-act-credit-assurance

An end-to-end self-assessment of a high-risk EU AI Act credit-scoring model across four properties (recourse, fairness, robustness, and a pre-registered explanation-faithfulness audit), with an Annex-IV-style governance pack, a signed audit opinion, and one-command reproduction. The faithfulness hypotheses were committed to a signed, timestamped record before the model was trained.
